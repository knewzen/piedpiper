Full Stack Web Developer (to Pied Maps Project)

Pied Maps is about more than just locations and directions, it's about modeling the physical world in software. In this position you will be developing internal software involved in curating, analyzing, and correcting the data which drives the public transit feature in Maps. This will include full stack development with a front-end focus. Key challenges include creating efficient workflows for data curation, optimizing database queries and modeling, and constructing views which provide insight into the complex data which makes up our transit network.


Key Qualifications

  • 5+ years of experience in Javascript and front-end web development frameworks / technologies
  • 5+ years of experience working with Java or newer JVM-based languages such as Scala or Clojure
  • Experience designing and implementing UIs for complex workflows and datasets
  • Ability to rapidly learn new technologies


Description
The Pied Maps data and engineering team is looking for a full stack web developer to join our team. We are constantly optimizing and improving our Transit data pipeline while introducing new features in order to deliver the highest-quality Transit experience to our customers.
